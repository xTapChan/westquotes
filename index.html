About this app

Get your daily dose of inspiration and confidence with WestQuotes. Every day, receive a new, iconic quote from Kanye West directly on your device.

Whether you need motivation, a fresh perspective, or just a glimpse into the mind of a visionary, WestQuotes has you covered. Save your favorite quotes, customize the app's theme to match your style, and set up home screen widgets so you never miss a word of genius.
